## What is Sahachari?
Sahachari (സഹചാരി) is your trusted everyday grocery and delivery companion for households in Kerala, India. The name means "companion" or "fellow traveller" in Malayalam. Sahachari connects you directly with local vendors, farmers, and home producers for fresh, quality products at fair prices, focusing especially on villages and rural communities across Kerala.

## What products does Sahachari sell?
Sahachari offers a wide range of daily grocery essentials including:
- Fresh vegetables: onions, tomatoes, potatoes, carrots, brinjal, ladies finger, capsicum, drumstick, raw banana, yam, pumpkin, beans, cucumber, cabbage, cauliflower, beetroot, radish, ginger, garlic
- Fresh fruits: apples, bananas, mangoes, oranges, grapes, papaya, pineapple, watermelon, guava, pomegranate, lemon, chikoo, jackfruit, kiwi
- Leafy greens: spinach, methi, coriander, palak, curry leaves, amaranth, fenugreek, mustard greens
- Dairy products: milk, curd, paneer, butter, ghee
- Grains and rice: various Kerala rice varieties, wheat flour (atta), dal, lentils, toor dal, moong dal
- Oils and spices: coconut oil, mustard oil, turmeric, pepper, cardamom, chilli powder, cumin, coriander powder, mustard seeds
- Groceries and daily essentials: dal, sugar, salt, tea, coffee, tamarind, coconut
- Snacks and ready-to-eat items
- Beverages: fresh juices, buttermilk, tender coconut water, tea, coffee
- Homemade food: curries, pickles, snacks from local home kitchens
- Fast food items: shawarma, sandwiches, rolls
- Services: dishwashing, cleaning, sweeping, mopping
- Rental items for household use

## Can the chatbot remind me to reorder my usual groceries every week?
Automatic reminders are not available yet. However, you can quickly repeat your usual grocery list anytime using the Reorder button on a past order in the Orders screen, or by telling the chatbot "reorder my last order."

## Can I ask the chatbot to save my usual order as a template?
While Sahachari does not yet have a dedicated saved template feature, you can use the wishlist to save items you order regularly, or use the Reorder button on a past order to quickly repeat your usual grocery list.

## Can I ask the chatbot to suggest a substitute for an out-of-stock item?
Yes, if an item you ask for is out of stock, you can ask the chatbot "what can I use instead of X?" and it will try to suggest a similar alternative based on what is currently available.

## What happens if I ask the chatbot for an item Sahachari does not sell?
If you ask for an item that Sahachari does not currently sell, the chatbot will inform you that it is unavailable. It may suggest a similar alternative product if one exists, or you can contact support to request the item be added in the future.

## Can I ask the chatbot for a shopping list based on a budget?
You can tell the chatbot your budget, for example "suggest groceries under 500 rupees," and it will try to recommend a combination of items that fits your budget based on current prices.

## Can I ask the chatbot which vegetables are currently cheapest?
You can ask the chatbot "which vegetables are cheapest right now?" and it will share current pricing information. Since prices depend on local vendor rates, the most affordable options can vary from day to day.

## Can the chatbot help me decide between two similar products?
If you are deciding between two similar items, you can ask the chatbot for details like price or availability for each, for example "is curd or paneer cheaper right now?" This can help you make a quicker decision.

## Can I ask the chatbot what is in season right now?
You can ask the chatbot what vegetables or fruits are currently in season. Availability depends on local vendor stock at the time of your order. Checking the products section in the app is also a good way to confirm current availability.

## How do I ask the chatbot to book a dishwashing service?
You can ask the chatbot something like "book a dishwashing service" or "show me services" and it will help you. You can also check the services section in the Sahachari app directly to see availability and book a convenient time slot.

## Can I ask the chatbot for vegetable combinations for a specific recipe?
Yes, mention the recipe name to the chatbot, for example "what vegetables do I need for avial?" and it will suggest the commonly used ingredients for that dish. You can then confirm and have them added to your cart.

## Can I ask the chatbot to hold my order for a few hours before confirming?
Items stay in your cart until you are ready. The order is only placed once you explicitly say "checkout" or "place my order," so you can take your time deciding before confirming.

## Can the chatbot help me plan meals for the whole week?
While the chatbot can help suggest ingredients for specific dishes you mention, a complete weekly meal planning feature is not currently available. You can ask it dish by dish and build your list that way.

## Is there a way to compare prices of different vegetables before ordering?
You can browse the products section in the Sahachari app to view prices of different vegetables side by side before deciding what to order. You can also ask the chatbot directly for the price of specific items.

## Can I ask the chatbot about nutritional value of a product?
You can ask the chatbot general questions like "is carrot good for eyes?" and it can share commonly known nutritional information. However, for specific dietary plans or medical conditions, it is best to consult a qualified nutritionist or doctor.

## Can I ask the chatbot to split my order into two deliveries?
Currently, Sahachari processes each order as a single delivery. If you need some items delivered at a different time, you will need to place separate orders for each batch.

## What if I do not know the exact quantity I need?
You can ask the chatbot for a suggestion based on your household size, for example "how much rice for a family of four?" Alternatively, you can order a smaller quantity first and place another order later if you need more.
